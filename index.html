Frequently Asked Questions

What is BiteRight?
BiteRight is an AI-powered gluten risk checker designed for people with coeliac disease and gluten intolerance. Simply scan a menu, meal, ingredient list, or barcode to get instant guidance on potential gluten risks.
How does the scanning work?
BiteRight uses advanced AI to analyze photos of menus, plated meals, or ingredient lists. Our AI identifies hidden gluten sources, cross-contamination risks, and local food practices to give you a safe, caution, or unsafe assessment.
What can I scan?
You can scan menus, plated meals, ingredient lists, or barcodes. BiteRight works with photos taken directly in the app or uploaded from your library. You can also type ingredients manually for quick checks.
Is BiteRight a medical device?
No, BiteRight is not a medical device. It's a guidance tool to help you assess gluten risks. Always double-check with kitchen staff, especially if you have coeliac disease. BiteRight provides helpful information but is not a substitute for professional medical advice.
What is Coeliac Safe Mode?
Coeliac Safe Mode is a stricter analysis setting that flags additional risks like cross-contamination, shared fryers, and hidden gluten sources. It's designed for people with diagnosed coeliac disease who need to be extra cautious about even trace amounts of gluten.
What are Chef Cards?
Chef Cards are digital cards that explain your dietary needs to restaurant staff in over 20 languages. They're designed to help you communicate your gluten requirements clearly when traveling or dining out, reducing language barriers and misunderstandings.
Why do you ask for my location?
BiteRight uses your location to localize ingredient knowledge and provide more accurate assessments. For example, soy sauce formulations vary by country‚Äîwhat's safe in Japan might not be safe in the UK. Setting your country helps us tailor our analysis to local food practices.
Is my data private?
Yes, privacy is important to us. Photos are analyzed for gluten assessment and are not stored long-term. We focus on privacy-first practices and don't sell your data. Your recent checks are stored locally on your device for your convenience.
What platforms is BiteRight available on?
BiteRight is currently available on iOS only. You can download it from the App Store. We're working to expand to other platforms in the future.
How accurate is BiteRight?
BiteRight uses advanced AI to provide helpful guidance, but it's not 100% guaranteed. Always verify with kitchen staff, especially if you have coeliac disease. The app is designed to help you make informed decisions, not replace your judgment or professional medical advice.
